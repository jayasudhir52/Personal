Replace Thermal Paste

Why Replace Thermal Paste?

  • Maintains optimal CPU/GPU temperatures by improving heat transfer.
  • Prevents overheating and thermal throttling.
  • Extends the lifespan of the processor and cooling components.
  • Necessary when upgrading or cleaning your PC after months/years of use.

Steps to Replace Thermal Paste

  • Power off the PC and unplug it from the outlet.
  • Remove the CPU cooler carefully following the manufacturerâ€™s instructions.
  • Clean old thermal paste from the CPU and cooler base using isopropyl alcohol and a lint-free cloth.
  • Apply a small pea-sized amount of new thermal paste in the center of the CPU.
  • Reinstall the CPU cooler evenly and securely.
  • Reconnect power and peripherals, then boot the system.
  • Monitor CPU/GPU temperatures to ensure proper cooling.

Additional Tips

Do not apply too much thermal paste, as it can reduce thermal conductivity. Regularly check temperatures if you notice overheating or performance drops.
