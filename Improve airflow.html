Improve Airflow

Why Improve Airflow?

  • Reduces temperatures of CPU, GPU, and other components.
  • Prevents thermal throttling and system slowdowns.
  • Extends lifespan of hardware components.
  • Helps maintain stable performance under heavy loads.

Steps to Improve Airflow

  • Organize and manage cables to prevent blockage of airflow.
  • Ensure intake and exhaust fans are properly positioned for optimal circulation.
  • Clean dust filters and internal components regularly.
  • Consider adding additional case fans if temperatures remain high.
  • Ensure there is enough space around the case for air to flow freely.
  • Check that fan orientations are correct (intake front/bottom, exhaust rear/top).

Additional Tips

Monitor component temperatures using software tools to ensure airflow improvements are effective. Using high-quality thermal paste and replacing aging fans can further enhance cooling efficiency.
