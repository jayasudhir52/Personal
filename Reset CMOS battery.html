Reset CMOS Battery

When to Reset CMOS

  • System fails to boot after BIOS changes.
  • Incorrect system time or BIOS settings.
  • Hardware changes are not detected.
  • Continuous boot loops or POST errors.

Steps to Reset CMOS Battery

  • Power off the computer and unplug it from the power source.
  • Press and hold the power button for 10 seconds to discharge residual power.
  • Open the computer case carefully.
  • Locate the round CMOS battery on the motherboard.
  • Remove the battery and wait for 5â€“10 minutes.
  • Reinsert the battery in the correct orientation.
  • Close the case and reconnect the power.

After Reset

Power on the system and enter the BIOS setup to reconfigure date, time, and any custom settings. If issues persist, the motherboard may require further diagnosis or replacement.
