Remove Unnecessary Startup Apps

Why Remove Startup Apps?

  • Reduces system boot time.
  • Improves overall system performance.
  • Frees up memory and CPU resources.
  • Prevents background apps from slowing down the system.

Steps to Disable Startup Apps (Windows)

  • Press Ctrl + Shift + Esc to open Task Manager.
  • Click the Startup tab.
  • Review the list of programs that start with Windows.
  • Select unnecessary applications.
  • Click Disable to prevent them from launching at startup.

After Disabling Startup Apps

Restart the system and observe improved boot time and responsiveness. Only keep essential programs enabled, such as antivirus software or hardware-related utilities.
