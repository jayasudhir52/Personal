Reset GPU & RAM

Why Reset GPU & RAM?

  • Fixes display or graphical glitches caused by hardware misalignment.
  • Helps resolve random crashes or system instability.
  • Clears temporary memory or GPU errors that affect performance.
  • Ensures proper contact between components and motherboard slots.

Steps to Reset GPU

  • Power off the PC and unplug it from the outlet.
  • Open the case carefully.
  • Unscrew and gently remove the GPU from the PCIe slot.
  • Check the PCIe slot and GPU contacts for dust or debris.
  • Reinsert the GPU firmly and secure it with screws.
  • Reconnect power cables and close the case.

Steps to Reset RAM

  • Power off the PC and unplug it from the outlet.
  • Open the case carefully.
  • Release the RAM modules by pressing the clips on each side.
  • Remove the RAM sticks and inspect the contacts for dust or debris.
  • Reinsert the RAM modules firmly until the clips click into place.
  • Close the case and reconnect power.

Additional Tips

Always ground yourself to prevent static discharge. If problems persist after reseating, consider testing components individually or checking for driver/firmware updates.
